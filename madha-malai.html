Madha Malai

Hill of Our Lady - A Sacred Place of Prayer and Pilgrimage

Welcome to Madha Malai

Madha Malai, meaning "Hill of Our Lady," is a sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Our Lady of Lourdes. This peaceful hilltop sanctuary offers pilgrims a unique opportunity to walk in the footsteps of faith, following the Stations of the Cross while ascending to the beautiful grotto of Our Lady.

The hill provides a serene environment for prayer, meditation, and spiritual reflection, away from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Many pilgrims have experienced profound spiritual moments and reported miraculous healings through the intercession of Our Lady of Lourdes.

Sacred Features

Stations of the Cross

Follow the path of Jesus through beautifully crafted stations along the hillside. Each station provides a moment for reflection on Christ's passion and sacrifice.

  • 14 Traditional Stations
  • Artistic stone carvings
  • Peaceful walking path
  • Meditation benches

Grotto of Our Lady

A replica of the famous Lourdes grotto where pilgrims come to pray and seek healing. The grotto is the centerpiece of Madha Malai and a place of profound spiritual significance.

  • Beautiful statue of Our Lady
  • Candle lighting area
  • Prayer request box
  • Quiet meditation space

Holy Water Spring

A natural spring blessed for the faithful to take holy water for their homes. Many believe in the healing properties of this sacred water.

  • Natural spring water
  • Blessed by priests
  • Available for pilgrims
  • Healing testimonies

Meditation Gardens

Peaceful gardens perfect for quiet reflection and prayer. The natural beauty of the surroundings enhances the spiritual experience of visitors.

  • Beautiful landscaping
  • Quiet seating areas
  • Native plants and flowers
  • Bird watching opportunities

Pilgrimage Guide

1

Begin at the Base

Start your pilgrimage at the base of the hill where you'll find the first Station of the Cross. Take a moment to prepare your heart for the spiritual journey ahead.

2

Follow the Stations

Walk slowly and prayerfully through each of the 14 Stations of the Cross. Allow time for reflection and prayer at each station.

3

Visit the Grotto

Arrive at the grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes. Light a candle, offer your prayers, and spend time in quiet contemplation before the statue of Our Lady.

4

Collect Holy Water

Visit the holy water spring and collect blessed water to take home. Many pilgrims use this water for blessing their homes and families.

5

Rest and Reflect

Spend time in the meditation gardens, reflecting on your pilgrimage experience and the graces received during your visit.

Pilgrimage Guidelines

Dress Code

  • Dress modestly and appropriately
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Bring a hat or cap for sun protection
  • Avoid revealing or inappropriate clothing

Conduct

  • Maintain silence and reverence
  • Respect other pilgrims' prayer time
  • Follow designated paths only
  • No smoking or alcohol consumption

Photography

  • Photography allowed in designated areas
  • No flash photography inside the grotto
  • Respect privacy of other pilgrims
  • No commercial photography without permission

Safety

  • Stay on marked paths
  • Supervise children at all times
  • Carry water, especially in hot weather
  • Report any emergencies to staff

How to Reach

Madha Malai is located adjacent to Our Lady of Lourdes Shrine in Chetpet. It's easily accessible by car, bus, or auto-rickshaw from Vellore city center.

Parking

Free parking is available at the base of the hill. Please park only in designated areas and follow the instructions of our volunteers.

Refreshments

Basic refreshments and drinking water are available at the base. We recommend bringing your own water bottle for the climb.

Accessibility

While the main path involves climbing, we have provisions for elderly and differently-abled pilgrims. Please contact us in advance for assistance.
