Mass Guidelines

Before Mass

  • Please arrive 10 minutes before mass begins
  • Dress modestly and appropriately
  • Switch off or silence mobile phones
  • Spend time in quiet prayer and preparation

During Mass

  • Maintain silence and reverence
  • Participate actively in prayers and songs
  • Follow the liturgical responses
  • Parents, please supervise your children

Holy Communion

  • Only baptized Catholics in good standing
  • Fast for at least one hour before communion
  • Receive with reverence and devotion
  • Spend time in thanksgiving after communion
