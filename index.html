Welcome to Our Sacred Home

Our Lady of Lourdes Shrine has been a beacon of faith and hope for over 125 years. Located in the heart of Chetpet, under the Vellore Diocese, our shrine welcomes pilgrims and devotees from all walks of life.

Experience the divine presence of Our Lady of Lourdes and find peace, healing, and spiritual renewal in this sacred space.
